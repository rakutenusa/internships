Favorite Project

I got the pleasure of working with both the iOS and android versions of Gateway. I tackled issues that retained user activity on older devices and made current shopping features even better. It exposed me to new technologies as well as new friendships within the team through collaboration. It is great knowing that the little time spent with the team are impactful differences to the daily lives of Rakuten users.

Biggest Takeaway

The biggest takeaway from Rakuten is surrounding myself with people that I enjoy working with, exposing myself to the different layers of technologies that makes Rakuten complex yet exciting, and realizing that staring at code all day is not too bad.

Favorite Internship Memory

My favorite internship memory was sharing the experience of drinking river water with a few team members during a team 11 mile hike. The road seemed endless while managing an insane level of thirst.
